About Us

Twitter Hashtag Analyser is an application for the Companies who wish to see how popular is their hashtag over the social networking sites such as twitter. It provides various services such as count the number of times the input hashtag has been used on the Twitter, view the top 10 user of the hashtag. Sentimental Analaysis of a hashtag and view the geographical locations of users using the hashtags. We intend to track the volume chart to see how the hashtags trend is and also show those social media profiles who uses those requested hashtag(s) most often.
